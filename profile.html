Kenya's Premier Cybersecurity Law Firm

Keniz Agira & Company Advocates is Kenya's leading law firm specializing exclusively in cybersecurity, digital forensics, and data protection law. We provide comprehensive legal solutions to protect organizations from digital threats while ensuring compliance with Kenya's Data Protection Act and international cybersecurity regulations. Our specialized practice combines deep legal expertise with technical understanding of digital forensics, incident response, and cyber risk management.

As the trusted legal partner for forward-thinking organizations, we serve businesses across sectors including financial institutions, technology companies, healthcare providers, government agencies, and multinational corporations. Our team is certified in digital forensics methodologies and stays ahead of evolving cyber threats through continuous training and strategic partnerships with cybersecurity technology firms.

Our Cybersecurity Specializations
