Our Cybersecurity Journey

Specializing in digital protection and evolving with the cyber threat landscape

2020

Cybersecurity Specialization Begins

Recognizing the growing digital threat landscape, we established Kenya's first dedicated cybersecurity legal practice. Focused initially on data protection compliance and basic digital risk management for corporate clients.

2022

Digital Forensics Expansion

Added certified digital forensics capabilities and established partnerships with cybersecurity technology firms. Expanded services to include incident response, digital evidence preservation, and cybercrime investigation support.

2023

Industry Recognition & Growth

Recognized as Kenya's leading cybersecurity law firm. Received awards for digital forensics excellence and expanded team to include specialized cyber litigation attorneys and data protection compliance experts.

2024

Advanced Cyber Capabilities

Implemented 24/7 cyber incident response center and advanced threat intelligence capabilities. Expanded services to include ransomware negotiation support, cryptocurrency transaction tracing, and advanced persistent threat (APT) investigation.

2025

Future-Ready Cybersecurity

Leading innovation in AI-powered threat detection legal frameworks, quantum computing security protocols, and IoT device compliance. Expanding regional presence while maintaining focus on cutting-edge cybersecurity legal solutions.

Our Cybersecurity Values

Principles that guide our digital protection practice

Proactive Protection

We believe in preventing cyber incidents before they occur through comprehensive risk assessments, robust security policies, and continuous monitoring strategies.

Digital Integrity

Maintaining chain of custody for digital evidence, ensuring data authenticity, and preserving evidentiary integrity throughout investigations and legal proceedings.

Data Confidentiality

Implementing the highest standards of data protection, ensuring client information remains secure and confidential throughout all legal processes and investigations.

Rapid Response

Providing immediate legal support during cybersecurity incidents to minimize damage, preserve evidence, and ensure regulatory compliance under pressure.

Technical Expertise

Combining legal knowledge with deep technical understanding of cybersecurity frameworks, digital forensics tools, and emerging threat vectors.

Collaborative Security
